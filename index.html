Full-Color Printing Services

01

PolyJet Full-Color

Stratasys J-series multi-material full-color printing. Simultaneous CMYK+W color with Shore 27A to rigid clear material combinations. Consumer product visualization, medical simulation, and architectural color models.

02

Binder Jet Full-Color

Full-color sandstone binder jetting for architectural models, figurines, and geographic terrain models. Cost-effective full color for large display pieces. 10 million color combinations without per-material changeover.

03

Hand-Painted SLA Finishing

High-resolution SLA print with professional airbrush painting service. Custom Pantone color matching, photo-realistic textures, and metallic finishes. Ideal for product visualization with the finest surface detail.
